And So The ENTERTAINMENT GUIDE Was Born.

My Favorite Recipes APP

Cooking can be an incredible experience but it can also be incredibly frustrating. Surfing through old cookbooks with stained hard to read recipe cards can be exhausting and take all the joy out of the kitchen. When one of my team members approached us with this idea, we knew we couldn't resist! The idea was wo create an app so that a user could store and access their favorite recipes in a one stop location. Enter Everyday Recipes, an easy to use recipe app that allows a user to add, store, edit, delete, the recipes they love.
